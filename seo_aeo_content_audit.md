## 🔴 Critical Gaps (Fix to enable AI citation)

1. **No TL;DR / Direct-Answer Blocks**
   - **Page**: All primary pages
   - **Signal**: Missing a 2-3 sentence direct-answer blockquote immediately following the H1.
   - **Impact**: AI search engines (like Perplexity or Google AI Overviews) cannot easily extract a self-contained summary to cite as a direct query answer.
   - **Fix**: Inject a high-level summary paragraph styled as a blockquote right below the page title headers.

2. **Missing FAQ Schema & Sections**
   - **Page**: All pages
   - **Signal**: No FAQ section (requires at least 4 entries) present.
   - **Impact**: Misses out on long-tail question-based query traffic and direct Q&A snippet extractions by voice and AI assistants.
   - **Fix**: Create an FAQ accordion block on the services pages with exactly 5 targeted, self-contained questions and answers.

3. **Missing Comparison Tables**
   - **Page**: Services Pages (`/ai-workflows`, `/web-dev`, `/mobile-apps`)
   - **Signal**: Missing structured table layouts comparing options, features, or "Before vs. After" metrics.
   - **Impact**: Search engines prioritize structured tables for commercial-intent queries. Relying solely on bullet points reduces comparison query rankings.
   - **Fix**: Introduce comparison tables outlining feature sets, tech stacks, or cost efficiencies.
